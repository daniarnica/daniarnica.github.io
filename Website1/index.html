Water Me Please is an organization created to put an end to anxiety and depression caused by stress in college students. By donating any given amount, you’re automatically helping a college student through some of the most stressful years of their lives. Every penny goes towards buying plants for students who suffer from anxiety or depression, and those plants will lift the students mood and immediately put them at ease. Ecotherapy is a large part of why we have things such as Central Park in the middle of New York City, and why we’re able to think clearer while taking a walk in the forest. It is proven that more than 62 percent of students withdrew from college due to mental health problems, such as anxiety and depression. According to surveys of college counselors, anxiety is the most common problem among students. Between the constant pressure of studying for their life long career, keeping up grades, taking care of themselves (mentally & physically), and maintaining healthy social lives, it’s no wonder such a large amount of students have dropped out of college due to mental health problems. While we are sure that our organization will help stressed out students, please see a doctor if you are experiencing extreme symptoms such as suicidal thoughts or any signs of physical illness.


While our society is so focused around this mentality that only medication can fix health issues, there is a much easier & simpler solution; ecotherapy. Ecotherapy has been proven to help boost moods and even save people from using medication. According to mind.org.uk, introducing just five people with mental health problems to ecotherapy saved the State more than $51,000 each year in costs for medication, Jobseeker’s Allowance, and healthcare. Ecotherapy not only allows you to feel better mentally, but enables you to connect with your enviornment more. We are so caught up with who’s posting what on Facebook and Instagram that we forget about the simple things that we’re already given. Take a walk, breathe in some fresh air, and inspire yourself to help out a stressed college student.
Every penny donated goes directly to buying plants for students struggling with anxiety, depression, or stress.
